GEES Policy Information

Refund Policy

GEES provides education counselling, admissions guidance, visa support, and related student services. Refund eligibility depends on the service, payment method, work already completed, and the terms agreed for the individual case.

Before requesting a refund

  • Keep your payment receipt, application reference, and any relevant agreement available.
  • Contact GEES with a clear description of the payment and the reason for the request.
  • Third-party charges, including university, visa, courier, insurance, or government fees, may be subject to the third party's own terms.

How GEES reviews requests

GEES reviews refund requests against the applicable agreement, payment record, and completed service work. A written response is provided after the relevant information has been reviewed.
