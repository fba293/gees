Frequently asked questions

When should I start my study abroad application?

Start as early as possible before your preferred intake so there is enough time for course selection, documents, admission, visa preparation, accommodation and travel planning.

Can GEES help me choose a country and course?

Yes. GEES reviews your academic background, budget, English score, timeline and career goals before recommending suitable study options.

Do you support students after admission?

Yes. Support can continue through visa file preparation, accommodation planning, insurance guidance, flight ticketing and pre-departure steps.

Not sure which service you need?

Tell GEES your profile, destination and timeline. We will guide you to the right next step.
